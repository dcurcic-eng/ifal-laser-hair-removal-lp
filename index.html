HOW IT WORKS
Light in,hair out.

The Alexandrite laser targets pigment inside each follicle and disables its growth structures. Moveo motion and contact cooling make every pass completely painless, with no stinging snap.

Goodto know.
Does laser hair removal hurt?

No. Thanks to the Motus AZ+ Moveo technology and built-in contact cooling, the treatment is painless. Most clients feel only a gentle warmth and stay comfortable the whole session, with no stinging snap.

How many sessions will I need?

Most clients see up to 90% reduction after six to eight sessions, with quick touch-ups every 12–18 months to maintain smoothness.

Is it safe for darker skin tones?

Yes. The Motus AZ+ Alexandrite laser with contact cooling is safe and effective for all Fitzpatrick skin types I–VI.

Can I shave between sessions?

Yes, you may shave or trim anytime. Avoid waxing, tweezing, or electrolysis, which remove the follicle and make the laser less effective.

How should I prepare?

Avoid sun and tanning beds for two weeks, shave the area 24 hours before, and skip retinoids on the treatment area for three days prior.
